Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

English

NetDiagnostics does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

  • Network diagnostic data (IP addresses, hostnames, ping results, traceroutes, DNS queries, port scans) is generated and displayed locally on your device. It is never sent to any server.
  • Report export (PDF/HTML) saves files to your device's local storage. You control where reports are saved and shared.
  • In-App Purchase (Premium unlock) is processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. The app does not receive, store, or transmit any payment information.
  • Crash logs are handled by Apple's built-in crash reporting (if you have opted in via iOS Settings > Privacy > Analytics). The app does not include any third-party analytics or tracking.
  • No advertising, no tracking. This app does not display ads and does not track your activity.

If you have questions about this policy, contact the developer via the support link below.

NetDiagnostics 不收集、存储或传输任何个人信息。

  • 崩溃日志由Apple内置的崩溃报告功能处理（如您已在iOS设置>隐私>分析中选择加入）。本App不包含任何第三方分析或追踪。
